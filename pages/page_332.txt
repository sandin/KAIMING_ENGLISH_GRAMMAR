11.33. Active and Passive Nn.—Although this chapter
deals exclusively with aspects of vbb., it should be point-
ed out that nn. and adjj. often involve or imply active or
passive action also. The English suffix -er or -or denotes
the doer of a deed, while the suffix -ee denotes the receiver
or the party affected by the action. Contrast—

employer — employee
payer — payee
addresser — addressee

lessor — lessee
vendor — vendee
