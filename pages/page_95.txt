Rarely we find an indirect question put in the form
of a direct question:—

The servant opened the door and told me his master was out.
He said the master had left word to say that he would be at the
club from four to half past five this afternoon, and would I be
willing to go and meet him there.

which express a command, a wish, or a conjecture, and
state what may be true rather than what is true, or what
the speaker wishes to be done rather than what is done.
