Time in Nouns

13.90. Time in Nouns.—Nn. do not, as a rule, involve
any notion of time. There are, however, some cases like
the following:—

her ex-husband                      the former president
an ex-president                    the bride-to-be
an ex-convict                      his mother-in-law-to-be
