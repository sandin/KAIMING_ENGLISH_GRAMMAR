## 第六章 重量、价值、尺寸、形状与方位

### 6.10. 此类范畴的国家差异
The English expressions of weight, value, size, distance, position and shape differ quite considerably from the Chinese.
英语中关于重量、价值、尺寸、距离、方位和形状的表达与中文存在显著差异。

One should learn to use these expressions correctly and to know their exact meaning.
我们应当学会正确使用这些表达并理解其确切含义。

### 6.20. 重量表达
Expressions of Weight.
重量表达

There are in measures of weight, as in measures of size and distance, two systems, one the old English system which is in general use, and the other the so-called "metric" system, more in scientific use.
与尺寸和距离的度量一样，重量度量也存在两种体系：一种是普遍使用的旧英制，另一种是更多用于科学领域的所谓"公制"。

The metric system is quite simple.
公制非常简单。

Both gram and gramme may be used in all these words.
在所有这类词汇中，gram和gramme均可使用。

- gram = about 15 Troy grains
  克 ≈ 15金衡格令

- decagram = 10 grams
  十克 = 10克

- hectogram = 100 grams
  百克 = 100克

- kilogram = 1,000 grams
  千克 = 1,000克

- decigram = 10th of 1 gram
  分克 = 1克的十分之一

- centigram = 100th of 1 gram
  厘克 = 1克的百分之一

- milligram = 1,000th of 1 gram
  毫克 = 1克的千分之一

The most common term is kilogram which is about 2.2 ordinary pounds.
最常用的术语是千克，约等于2.2常衡磅。

In the old English system, there are again two series of weight measures: one the common weight, or avoirdupois (pr. [,ævədʊ'pɔɪz]) weight, and the other used by gold- and silversmiths, called Troy weight.
在旧英制中，又存在两种重量度量系列：一种是常衡，另一种是金银匠使用的金衡。

The avoirdupois pound is heavier than the Troy pound, and is equal to 12 Chinese ounces.
常衡磅比金衡磅更重，相当于12中国两。

Study the following equivalents (the abbreviations are put in brackets):—
学习以下等价关系（缩写置于括号内）：

Common Weight (avoir.)
常衡

- 16 grams = 1 ounce (oz.)
  16格令 = 1盎司

- 16 ounces = 1 pound (lb.) = 7,000 grains
  16盎司 = 1磅 = 7,000格令

- 112 pounds = 1 hundredweight (cwt.)
  112磅 = 1英担

- 2,240 pounds = 1 ton
  2,240磅 = 1吨

- 14 pounds = 1 stone (used in speaking of a man's weight)
  14磅 = 1英石（用于描述人体重）

Troy Weight
金衡

- 24 grains = 1 pennyweight (dwt.)
  24格令 = 1英钱

- 20 pennyweights = 1 ounce (oz.)
  20英钱 = 1盎司

- 12 ounces = 1 pound (lb.) = 5,760 grains
  12盎司 = 1磅 = 5,760格令

Both pounds, ounces and pound, ounce are used for the plural, but now it is much more common to say pounds, ounces, while stone is more common than stones for the plural (He weighs twelve stone / a man of twelve stone).
pounds, ounces和pound, ounce都可用于复数形式，但现在更常说pounds, ounces，而stone的复数形式比stones更常见（他重十二英石/一个十二英石重的人）。

Notice that in all expressions of weight, size, value, distance, we use the terms of measure immediately after the vbb. weigh, measure, cost and the adj. worth.
注意在所有重量、尺寸、价值、距离的表达中，度量术语紧接在动词weigh、measure、cost和形容词worth之后。

The package weighs a pound and half.
这个包裹重一磅半。

It costs three dollars.
它价值三美元。

It is worth the money.
它物有所值。

It is not worth sixpence.
它不值六便士。

The bed measures six feet long.
这张床量得六英尺长。

Compare also expressions of time and number like It lasts seven minutes, The troops numbered fifteen thousand and also the expressions, weighs heavy, weighs much, weighs little, is worth much, is worth little, cost dear, measure long, measure short, etc.
同时比较时间和数量的表达，如：持续七分钟，部队数量达一万五千，以及表达：称起来很重，很重，很轻，很值钱，不值钱，价格昂贵，量得长，量得短等。

### 6.30. 价值表达
Expressions of Value.
价值表达

In English, there are two different ways of counting money, the English and the American way.
英语中有两种不同的货币计数方式：英式和美式。

The English way is really as complicated as, and only a little less crazy than, the Chinese way of counting dollars and taels and "big money" and "small money."
英式方式实际上与中文计算元、两、"大洋"和"小洋"的方式一样复杂，只是稍微不那么令人困惑。

However, there are only three chief units to learn, as follows:—
然而，只需学习三个主要单位，如下所示：

- 1 penny = about 2 gold cents
  1便士 ≈ 2金美分

- 12 pence = 1 shilling = about 25 cts. Gold
  12便士 = 1先令 ≈ 25金美分

- 20 shillings = 1 pound = about $5.00 Gold
  20先令 = 1英镑 ≈ 5.00金美元

Crown and sovereign are names of English coins.
克朗和沙弗林是英国硬币的名称。

- 1 crown = 5 shillings
  1克朗 = 5先令

- 1 halfcrown = 2 shillings and sixpence
  1半克朗 = 2先令6便士

- 1 sovereign = 1 pound
  1沙弗林 = 1英镑

The symbols used are:—
使用的符号如下：

  £. s. d. = 英镑—先令—便士

- £6.10.6. = six pound ten and six (ten shillings and sixpence)
  £6.10.6. = 六英镑十先令六便士

- £3 = three pounds
  £3 = 三英镑

- 10/6 = ten and six (ten shillings and sixpence)
  10/6 = 十先令六便士

- 4/- = four shillings
  4/- = 四先令

- 1/- = one shilling
  1/- = 一先令

- 8d. or -/8 = eightpence
  8d. 或 -/8 = 八便士

The terms pound and penny require special notice.
pound和penny这两个术语需要特别注意。

- five pounds (-s usual)
  五英镑（通常加-s）

- five pound ten (without -s)
  五英镑十先令（不加-s）

- five pounds sterling (sterling used only with exact sums of pounds, as £500 stg., but not "five pound ten stg.")
  五英镑（sterling仅用于精确的英镑数额，如£500 stg.，但不用于"five pound ten stg."）

- pennies (plural as regards coins)
  pennies（指硬币的复数）

- pence (plural as regards sum or value)
  pence（指金额或价值的复数）

- halfpenny (pr. ['heipni])
  半便士

- twopence (pr. ['tæpns])
  两便士

- threepence (pr. ['θri:pns])
  三便士

- twopenny piece (pr. ['tæpəni])
  两便士硬币

- threepenny piece (pr. ['θri:pəni])
  三便士硬币

- fourpence to elevenpence and twentypence written together (pr. [-pns])—otherwise written separately (pr. [pens]), e.g., thirteen pence
  四便士到十一便士以及二十便士连写（发音为[-pns]）——否则分开写（发音为[pens]），例如，十三便士

The American dollar-and-cents system is like the Chinese.
美式的元分制与中国的类似。

- 100 cents (cts.) = 1 dollar ($1.00)
  100分 = 1元

- 10 cents = 1 dime
  10分 = 1角

- 5 cents = 1 nickel
  5分 = 1镍币

For the following symbols read as follows:—
以下符号读法如下：

- $1.00 (U.S.) : one dollar U.S. currency
  $1.00（美国）: 一美元（美国货币）

The English terms for the Chinese currency are as follows:—
中国货币的英文术语如下：

- $1.00 (Mex.)    : one dollar Mex. (or Mexican)
  $1.00（墨元）   : 一墨元

- ¥ 25 or 25 tls. : twenty-five taels
  ¥ 25 或 25 tls. : 二十五两

- 10 Hk. tls.     : ten Haikwan taels
  10 Hk. tls.     : 十海关两

- ¥ 0.03          : three tael cents
  ¥ 0.03          : 三分银

- 23 cop. or cops.: twenty-three coppers
  23 cop. 或 cops.: 二十三文

- cash = 文; 現洋
  文；現洋

- big money = 大洋
  大洋

- small money = 小洋
  小洋

Among the non-English foreign currencies, we may mention here the German mark (=25 cents Gold) and the French franc (=20 cents Gold).
在非英语的外国货币中，我们可以提及德国马克（=25金美分）和法国法郎（=20金美分）。

We speak also of paper mark, gold mark and paper franc, gold franc.
我们也说纸币马克、金马克和纸币法郎、金法郎。

Notice also expressions like a dollar's worth of salt-eggs, fifteen-cents' worth of sugar, a dollar note (banknote) and a five-pound note.
同时注意以下表达：价值一美元的咸蛋，价值十五美分的糖，一元纸币（钞票）和五英镑纸币。

### 6.40. 尺寸与距离表达
Expressions of Size and Distance.
尺寸与距离表达

There are also two systems here, the old English system and the metric system.
这里也有两种体系：旧英制和公制。

In the metric system, the meter here is used as the basis, and the other names have prefixes like deca-, kilo-, deci-, milli-, which have the same meaning as in the "gram" series (cf. §6.20).
在公制中，米被用作基础单位，其他名称带有前缀，如 deca-、kilo-、deci-、milli-，其含义与"克"系列中的相同（参见§6.20）。

The practically useful words here are:—
实际有用的词汇如下：

- meter = 39 inches, a little over a yard
  米 = 39英寸，略超过一码

- kilometer = 1,000 meters, about two-thirds of a mile
  千米 = 1,000米，约三分之二英里

- centimeter = 100th of a meter, about 1/3 of an inch
  厘米 = 百分之一米，约三分之一英寸

In the old system, there are the following expressions:
在旧制中，有以下表达：

- inch = 英寸, about 0.73 Chinese in.
  英寸 ≈ 0.73中国寸

- foot = 12 inches, about 8.7 Chinese in.
  英尺 = 12英寸 ≈ 8.7中国寸

- yard = 3 feet = 2.6 Chinese ft.
  码 = 3英尺 = 2.6中国尺

- mile = 1,760 yds., about 3.3 Chinese li
  英里 = 1,760码 ≈ 3.3中国里

- furlong = 220 yds., or 8th of a mile
  弗隆 = 220码，或八分之一英里

- fathom = 6 ft. or 2 yds. (especially in measuring depth of water)
  英寻 = 6英尺或2码（尤用于测量水深）

- 5′ 4″ = five feet four inches
  5′ 4″ = 五英尺四英寸

For the plural, we almost always add an -s to these terms, except when they are used as modifiers, as in a five-foot shelf, a two-yard stick, three inch (or inches) wide.
对于复数形式，我们几乎总是在这些术语后加-s，除非它们用作修饰语，如：一个五英尺长的架子，一根两码长的棍子，三英寸宽。

Learn also the following expressions of size, and surface and cubic measures:—
同时学习以下关于尺寸、面积和体积度量的表达：

- four square feet (4 sq. ft.) = four squares, each of which is one foot long and one foot wide; three sq. ft. = three such squares = 三方尺
  四平方英尺 = 四个正方形，每个一英尺长一英尺宽；三平方英尺 = 三个这样的正方形 = 三方尺

- four feet square = a square whose sides are all four feet long = 四尺見方
  四英尺见方 = 一个边长均为四英尺的正方形 = 四尺见方

- square mile = 方哩
  平方英里

- acre = 4,840 sq. yds. = 英畝 (the Chinese mow is a highly shifting measure)
  英亩 = 4,840平方码 = 英亩（中国的"亩"是一个变化很大的度量单位）

- three feet long by four feet wide, or three by four (often written 3′×4′)
  三英尺长四英尺宽，或三乘四（常写作3′×4′）

- four cubic feet = 四立方尺
  四立方英尺

- twenty cubic centimeters
  二十立方厘米

Notice that we can say a thing is the size of . . . , is twice (half) the size of . . .
注意我们可以说某物的大小是……，是……的两倍（一半）大。

Notice also that we very commonly say A man is a hundred yards off, The water is four inches deep, using a hundred yards and four inches as adverb phrases, and the word off as an adjective.
同时注意我们很常说：一个人在百码外，水四英寸深，这里"a hundred yards"和"four inches"用作副词短语，而"off"用作形容词。

### 6.50. 形状表达：英语的形状盲
Expressions of Shape: English Shape-Blindness.
形状表达：英语的形状盲

A great difference is noticeable when we compare the English and Chinese notions of shape.
比较英语和中文的形状概念时，可以注意到一个巨大的差异。

Whereas the Chinese seem unable to think of a thing without calling up its shape also, the English language, looked at from the purely Chinese point of view, seems to be spoken by a race of shape-blind people.
中国人似乎思考事物时不能不联想到其形状，而从纯粹中文的视角看，说英语的人似乎是一个形状盲的种族。

This is another instance of the highly concrete and synthetic way of Chinese thinking.
这是中国人高度具体和综合思维方式的又一个例证。

English suffices with a fish, a table, a bookcase, an ox, etc.
